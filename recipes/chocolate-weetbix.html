Chocolate Protein Weetbix

Macros: 404cals | 35.9g Protein | 34.3g Carbs | 11g Fat

Wake up strong with this chocolate protein ovenight Weetbix recipe! Find it here.

Ingredients

  • 35g (approx. 2 pcs.) Weetbix
  • 32g (1 scoop) chocolate protein powder
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) water
  • 120g greek yoghurt
  • Sweetener of choice

Procedure

  1. Crush the weetbix.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the protein powder and water.
  3. Add the protein mixture.
  4. Combine the yoghurt + sweetener.
  5. Add the yoghurt on top.
  6. Add toppings.
  7. Leave in the fridge overnight.
